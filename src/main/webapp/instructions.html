Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Provide Initial Company Details

  1. Name of the Company: Enter the official name of your company.
  2. Street Name and House Number: Include any additional address details.
  3. Start Date: Indicate the date your company will begin operations.
  4. Phone Number: Provide a contact number for your company.
  5. Website (Optional): Enter your company's website if you have one (most new companies may not).

Step 2: Provide Personal Information

  1. Name: Enter your full name.
  2. Address: Provide your current residential address.
  3. Birthdate: Enter your date of birth.
  4. BSN: Fill in your burgerservicenummer (BSN) - citizen service number.

Step 3: Describe Your Company’s Services

  1. Company Services: Briefly explain what your company will do. For example, if you are a computer science student, you might write: “Providing software solutions for companies.”

Step 4: Financial Projections

  1. Expected Earnings: Estimate your company’s first-year earnings. A typical figure is around 5000 euros.
  2. Expected Expenditures: Estimate the amount of money you expect to spend on your company in the first year. This is usually zero, as most companies you work for will provide the necessary resources.
  3. Number of Employees: Enter at least 3 employees to expect working for your company.
  4. Official Correspondence Address: If you do not have an office, use your home address for all tax authority correspondence.

Step 5: Provide Personal and Contact Information

  1. Initials and Surname: Enter your initials and surname.
  2. Street Name and House Number: Include any additional address details.
  3. Postal Code and City: Fill in your postal code and city.
  4. Phone Number: Provide your contact number.
  5. Date of Signature: Enter the date you are signing the form.
  6. Signature: Sign the form to complete the registration.
