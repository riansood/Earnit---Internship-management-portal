Help Page

  • EarnIt simplifies the process of becoming self-employed for international students. Our platform automates self-employment form filling, assists with bookkeeping and tax calculations, and sends timely reminders for tax filings. Students can easily sign up, apply for permits, report earnings, and download necessary documents. EarnIt staff can manage and verify applications efficiently.

    Filling Self-Employment Permits:
    Q: Can I save my progress and continue filling out the forms later?
    A: Unfortunately, you can't save your progress and return to the form later. Please make sure to complete the entire form in one session, as your information will not be saved until you submit it.

    Q: What information do I need to enter when reporting my earnings?
    A: When reporting your earnings, please include the following information:

    1.Your earnings for each quarter.

    2.The dates you received each payment.


    Email Notifications:

    Q: How often will I receive email reminders if I haven't filed my tax return?

    A: If you haven't noted in the system that your tax return was filed, you'll receive email reminders at various intervals to help ensure you meet the deadline. These reminders will be sent 1 month, 2 weeks, 1 week, 3 days, and 1 day before the deadline.


    Q: Can I opt out of receiving email reminders?

    A: Once you've completed and filed your tax return, you have the option to opt out of receiving email reminders from your dashboard. The system will respect your preference and refrain from sending reminders if you choose to opt out.



    Q: What is a VAT number?


    A: A VAT number (Value Added Tax number) is a unique identification number assigned to businesses that are registered for VAT purposes. It serves as a way for authorities to track and monitor a company's transactions and tax obligations related to the buying and selling of goods and services. Businesses use their VAT number when invoicing customers and when reporting VAT to the tax authorities. It's essential for ensuring compliance with VAT regulations and for conducting business transactions within the European Union (EU) and internationally.
